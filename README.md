## 🌟 Problem Statement
"Traditional tutoring services are often expensive, inconvenient, or unavailable outside of fixed hours, leaving students without adequate support when they need it most. This gap hinders academic performance, increases stress, and discourages independent learning."


## 💡 What it does
"*Collab* is an innovative tutoring platform designed to make learning affordable, flexible, and engaging. With AI chatbot support, interactive study rooms, live tutoring, and a rewards system, students gain instant help, collaborate effectively, and stay motivated."


## 🚀 Main Features
🧠 ML-Based Recommendations – Personalized tutor and resource matching using machine learning, based on students’ questionnaires and their activity on the platform.

🤖 AI Academic Helper – Instant help & smart recommendations.

💬 Study Rooms – Public/private group study where they could have their discussion

🎓 Qualified Tutors – Verified through a test and identity verification before offering tutoring.

💰 Collab Coins Marketplace – Token economy for tutoring & rewards.

## 🔄 User Flow
1. Student signs up and completes a short questionnaire.  
2. ML system recommends suitable tutors and resources.  
3. Student joins a study room or chats with the AI chatbot.  
4. Tutors provide help in real-time.  
5. Students earn Collab Coins as rewards.


## ⚙️ Mock Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** React + Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Flask
- **Database:** PostgreSQL
- **Design & Prototype:** Figma
- **AI/ML:** Python (TensorFlow) for recommendations
